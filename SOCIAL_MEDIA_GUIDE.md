# RateMyBaliBuilder - Instagram & TikTok Guide

## The Goal

Build a following of expats and investors interested in Bali property. Convert followers ‚Üí website visitors ‚Üí paying members.

**Not vanity metrics.** We don't need 100K followers. We need 5,000 engaged people who might build in Bali.

---

## Platform Strategy

| Platform | Audience | Content Style | Posting Frequency |
|----------|----------|---------------|-------------------|
| **Instagram** | 30-50yr expats, investors, lifestyle seekers | Polished, informative, aspirational | 4-5x/week |
| **TikTok** | 25-40yr, more casual, discovery-driven | Raw, entertaining, hook-driven | 5-7x/week |

**Start with Instagram.** Our audience skews older and higher income. TikTok is bonus reach.

---

## Content Pillars (The 5 Types of Posts)

### 1. Horror Stories (40% of content)
**Why it works:** Fear sells. People searching for builders are terrified of getting scammed.

**Examples:**
- "This villa looked finished... until monsoon season" (before/after water damage)
- "He paid $200K and the contractor disappeared"
- "3 red flags this investor ignored"
- "Why your builder wants cash payments (and why you shouldn't)"

**Format:** Carousel (swipe for the reveal), Reel with dramatic music, screenshot of chat with scammer

---

### 2. Builder Spotlights (20% of content)
**Why it works:** Shows we have real data. Builds trust. Builders might share it.

**Examples:**
- "This builder has 12 five-star reviews. Here's why."
- "We verified this contractor's last 5 projects"
- "Recommended builder of the week"
- Interview clips with verified builders

**Format:** Reel (walkthrough of their project), carousel (project photos + stats)

---

### 3. Educational Tips (20% of content)
**Why it works:** Positions us as experts. SEO value when cross-posted to blog.

**Examples:**
- "5 questions to ask before signing a contract"
- "What does PBG actually mean?" (permit explainer)
- "Leasehold vs Freehold in 60 seconds"
- "How much does it cost to build a villa in 2025?"
- "Why your quote is missing these 3 things"

**Format:** Talking head Reel, carousel with tips, infographic

---

### 4. Behind the Scenes (10% of content)
**Why it works:** Humanizes the brand. Shows we're on the ground.

**Examples:**
- "We just visited this construction site in Canggu"
- "Calling a builder to verify their license"
- "Day in the life: vetting Bali contractors"
- "Our team checking a project handover"

**Format:** Raw Reel, Stories, casual photo dump

---

### 5. Wins & Social Proof (10% of content)
**Why it works:** Shows the platform works. Encourages reviews.

**Examples:**
- "Another investor found their builder through us"
- Screenshot of thank-you DM
- "84 builders vetted. 36 reviews. Growing every week."
- "This review just saved someone $50K"

**Format:** Screenshot post, celebration Reel, milestone graphic

## Viral Triggers (What Makes Content Spread)

### 1. Outrage
Content that makes people angry gets shared. Scam stories, bad builders, unfair situations.

### 2. "I didn't know that"
Educational content that makes people feel smart when they share it.

### 3. Saves
Practical tips people want to reference later. Checklists, red flags, questions to ask.

### 4. "This happened to me too"
Relatable experiences that prompt people to comment and tag friends.

### 5. Controversy
Takes that challenge common beliefs. "Unpopular opinion" format.

## Batch Content Creation

### Weekly Batching (2-3 hours)

**Step 1: Gather raw material**
- Screenshot DMs/reviews for social proof
- Save construction photos from site visits
- Note any horror stories heard that week

**Step 2: Create 5-7 posts**
- 2 carousels (horror story + tips)
- 2-3 Reels (talking head + walkthrough)
- 1-2 static posts (milestone + quote)

**Step 3: Write captions + hashtags**
- Keep in notes app, copy/paste when posting

**Step 4: Schedule**
- Use Meta Business Suite (free) for Instagram
- TikTok: draft and save, post manually

## Engagement Strategy

### Daily (10-15 min)
- Reply to all comments within 1 hour of posting

- Find and follow 20-30 relevant accounts (expat, Bali, real estate)

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Posting without a hook** - First 2 seconds decide everything
2. **Too polished** - Raw/authentic outperforms on TikTok
3. **No CTA** - Always tell them what to do next
4. **Inconsistent posting** - Algorithm rewards consistency
5. **Ignoring comments** - Engagement drives reach
6. **Only promotional content** - 80% value, 20% promotion
7. **Copying competitors exactly** - Adapt, don't clone
8. **Giving up after 30 days** - Takes 3-6 months to build momentum
