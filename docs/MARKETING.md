# Marketing & Growth Strategy

### Primary

- Remote workers investing in real estate

### Secondary
- Tourists planning long-term stays
- Property investors
- Real estate agents needing builder vetted
- Architects/designers needing builder referrals

## Launch Strategy

### Phase 1: Seed Database (Week 1-2)
**Goal:** 50+ builders in database before public launch

1. **Import Known Blacklist**
   - Scrape existing FB group warning posts
   - Document builders with multiple complaints
   - Add to "blacklisted" status with notes

2. **Add Recommended Builders**
   - Reach out to 30 trusted builders personally
   - Offer free "Verified Builder" status for first 30 days
   - Get their info: name, phone, Instagram, company

3. **Seed Reviews**
   - Ask 20 friends/network who've built in Bali
   - Offer $20 credit for first 100 reviews
   - Quality over quantity - need detailed reviews

### Phase 3: Review Collection Push (Month 2)
**Goal:** 500+ reviews, establish credibility

**Incentives:**
- $20 credit for every approved review
- Bonus $10 for reviews with photos
- Monthly prize: $100 credit for best review

**Tactics:**
- Post in FB groups asking for builder experiences
- Create Google Form for easy submissions
- Follow up with people who search but don't review

### Phase 4: Scale (Month 3+)
**Goal:** 5,000+ searches/month, 200+ paying builders

**Paid Ads:**
- Facebook Ads targeting Bali expats ($500/month budget)
- Google Ads for "Bali builder reviews" keywords
- Instagram sponsored posts

**SEO Optimization:**
- Write blog posts: "Top 10 Builders in Canggu"
- Builder guides: "How to Avoid Builder Scams"
- Rank for: "bali builder reviews", "trusted builder bali"

**Partnerships:**

- Architects (reciprocal promotion)
- Property management companies

**PR Push:**
- Reach out to Bali expat YouTubers

- Get featured in NOW! Bali magazine

## Content Strategy

### Weekly Content Calendar

**Monday:** Builder warning post (blacklisted)
**Wednesday:** Success story (recommended builder)
**Friday:** Construction tip/advice
**Weekend:** User review highlight

### Content Types

**High-Performing:**
- Builder scam stories (people love drama)
- Before/after construction photos
- "Red flags to watch for"
- "Questions to ask your builder"

**Educational:**
- Understanding Bali building permits
- Typical construction costs (Rp/sqm)
- Contract templates
- Legal protections

## Viral Growth Tactics

### 1. Controversy Magnet
Name and shame builders publicly (with evidence)
- Post blacklisted builders with full details
- Screenshots of complaints
- People will share to warn others

### 2. Free Value First
- Free builder checklists PDF
- Free contract template download
- Free initial search (first 50 users)

### 3. Referral Program
- Give $10 credit for every referral
- Friend gets $10 credit too
- Track with referral codes

### 4. FOMO Builders
- "Only 10 Verified Builder spots left this month"
- "Join 47 other verified builders"
- Social proof drives signups
