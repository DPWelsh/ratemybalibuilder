Logo Concepts Suite - Traffic Light / Rating Theme

Concept 1: The Rating House

Combines a simple house silhouette with traffic light indicators. Clean, literal, and professional.

Concept 2: Trusted Shield

Focuses on verification and trust. The green shield implies safety and "verified" status.

Concept 3: Construction Ratings

Uses construction imagery (hard hat) combined with review stars. Very literal and easy to understand.

Concept 4: Modern Abstract

A modern tech-startup feel. Uses blocks to form a 'B' shape while integrating the traffic light colors.

Concept 5: Minimal Typographic

Clean typography with subtle color accents. Best for header/nav bars where space is limited.

Recommendation

For a trust-based platform, Concept 2 (Trusted Shield) or Concept 5 (Minimal Typographic) usually works best.
- Concept 2 builds immediate trust with the shield iconography.
- Concept 5 is very clean and professional, similar to modern SaaS tools.
